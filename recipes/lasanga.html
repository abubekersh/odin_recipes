Classic Comfort: Hearty Layers of Savory Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe promises layers of rich, hearty flavors that meld perfectly with gooey cheese and savory sauce. It's a dish that invites warmth and comfort, making it ideal for family gatherings or cozy dinners at home. With each bite, you'll savor the harmonious blend of tender pasta, robust tomato sauce, creamy cheeses, and seasoned ground beef. Prepare to indulge in a satisfying meal that's both timeless and delicious.

ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 1 large egg
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  3. Assemble the Lasagna
  4. Bake the Lasagna
  5. Serve
