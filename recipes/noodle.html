Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a classic Thai street food dish that combines the bold flavors of tamarind, fish sauce, and lime with the satisfying texture of stir-fried rice noodles. It's typically made with shrimp or chicken, but can also be prepared as a vegetarian dish. This vibrant and flavorful meal is garnished with crunchy peanuts, fresh bean sprouts, and a squeeze of lime, making it a favorite for those who enjoy a balance of sweet, sour, salty, and umami tastes.

Ingredients

  • 8oz rice noodels
  • 2 cloves garlic, mince
  • 1/2 cup frim tofu, cubed
  • 1/2 cup shrimp or chicken
  • 1 cuo bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
