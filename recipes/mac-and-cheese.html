Creamy Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this creamy baked macaroni and cheese. Each forkful promises a velvety blend of tender pasta enveloped in a rich, cheesy sauce. This dish is perfect for any occasion, from family dinners to potluck gatherings, offering a satisfying taste of nostalgia and warmth in every bite.

ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups (960ml) whole milk
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Make the Cheese Sauce
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  4. Bake the Mac and Cheese
  5. Serve
